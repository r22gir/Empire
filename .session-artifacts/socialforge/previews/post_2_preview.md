## Caption
The difference custom drapery makes.

This living room in Silver Spring, MD had builder-grade blinds that were doing nothing for the space. The homeowner wanted something that would make the room feel taller, warmer, and more put-together — without going overly formal.

We recommended floor-length pinch pleat panels in a heavyweight linen blend with a standard white lining. The rod was mounted 4 inches below the ceiling to maximize the visual height of the room.

The result speaks for itself. Swipe to see the before.
