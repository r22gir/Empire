## Caption
Custom floating shelves, installed in a home office in Georgetown, DC.

The client needed shelving that worked around an asymmetric wall with a window on one side. Off-the-shelf wasn't an option — nothing fit. So we designed a set of floating shelves in white oak, graduated in length, with concealed mounting hardware.

Here's what went into this build:
- Material: 8/4 white oak, planed and jointed in-house
- Finish: matte polyurethane clear coat, two coats sanded between
- Mounting: French cleat system hidden inside each shelf — rated for 75 lbs per shelf
- Install time: 3 hours on-site

The result: clean lines, no visible brackets, and a wall that finally works for the space. The client uses them for books, plants, and display pieces.

Custom built-ins solve the problems that standard furniture can't. If you have an awkward space, an unusual wall, or a specific vision — that's exactly what we're here for.
