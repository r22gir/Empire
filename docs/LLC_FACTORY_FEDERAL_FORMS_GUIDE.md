## 1. US DISTRICT COURTS (Federal)

### General Federal Court Fee Schedule (All Districts)

| Service | Fee |
|---|---|
| Certification of any document | $12 |
| Exemplification of any document (triple-sealed) | $24 |
| Paper reproduction | $0.50/page |
| Electronic document reproduction (PACER) | $0.10/page (max 30 pages charge) |

### Form: PACER Account Registration
- **Agency:** Administrative Office of the US Courts
- **URL:** https://pacer.uscourts.gov/
- **Fee:** $0.10/page; no charge if quarterly usage stays under $30
- **Client data needed:** Name, address, email, phone, payment method
- **Processing time:** Immediate electronic access once registered

### Form: Certified Copy (AO 451) / Exemplification Certificate
- **Agency:** US Courts (all districts)
- **URL:** https://www.uscourts.gov/forms-rules/forms/certified-copy
- **Exemplification:** https://www.uscourts.gov/forms-rules/forms/exemplification-certificate-district-court
- **Fee:** $12 (certified) or $24 (exemplified/triple-sealed) + $0.50/page reproduction
- **Client data needed:** Case number, document description, party names, date of filing
- **Processing time:** 3-10 business days

### US District Court for the District of Columbia

- **Phone:** (202) 354-3000
- **Email:** records@dcd.uscourts.gov

- **URL:** https://www.dcd.uscourts.gov/court-records
- **Fee:** Standard federal schedule ($12 cert / $24 exemplified)

### Eastern District of Virginia (EDVA)
- **Form:** Copy Request Form
- **URL:** https://www.vaed.uscourts.gov/sites/vaed/files/Copy%20Request%20Form_1.pdf
- **Forms page:** https://www.vaed.uscourts.gov/forms/
- **Payment:** Cash (exact change), check, money order, major credit cards

### District of Maryland

- **Fee:** $12 certification / $24 exemplification + $0.50/page

## 2. DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE (DOJ) / FBI

### Form: FD-1164 — Identity History Summary Request
- **Agency:** FBI / CJIS Division
- **URL:** https://www.fbi.gov/file-repository/cjis/fd-1164-identity-history-summary-request-110120.pdf/view
- **Fee:** $18 per request
- **Payment (mail):** Money order, certified check, or cashier's check payable to "Treasury of the United States"
- **Payment (online):** Credit card via FBI eDO system
- **Client data needed:** Full name, DOB, place of birth, SSN, current address, sex, race, fingerprints
- **Processing time:** 1-3 business days (electronic); 12-14 weeks (mail-in)
- **Online portal:** https://www.edo.cjis.gov

### Form: FD-258 — Applicant Fingerprint Card
- **Agency:** FBI
- **URL:** https://www.fbi.gov/file-repository/cjis/fd-258.pdf/view
- **Fee:** Free (card itself); fingerprinting fee varies $5-$40
- **Note:** FD-258 for employment/licensing; FD-1164 for personal Identity History Summary
- **Client data needed:** Name, aliases, DOB, SSN, citizenship, employer info, reason

### FBI-Approved Channelers (Expedited Background Checks)
- **URL:** https://www.fbi.gov/how-we-can-help-you/more-fbi-services-and-information/identity-history-summary-checks/list-of-fbi-approved-channelers-for-departmental-order-submissions
- **Fee:** $18 FBI fee + channeler processing fee
- **Processing time:** 24-72 hours (electronic)
- **Key channelers:** Accurate Biometrics, Biometrics4All, Fieldprint, First Advantage, IDEMIA, IdentoGO, National Background Check Inc.

### Form: DOJ-361 — Certification of Identity (FOIA)
- **Agency:** Department of Justice
- **URL:** https://www.justice.gov/ust/file/doj361_form.pdf/dl
- **Fee:** Free to submit
- **Client data needed:** Full name, citizenship status, SSN, current address, DOB, place of birth, handwritten signature
- **Warning:** False statements subject to criminal penalties under 18 U.S.C. 1001

### FBI eFOIA Request (Online)
- **URL:** https://forms.fbi.gov/fbi-efoia-request-form
- **Fee:** Free
- **Status check:** (540) 868-1535 or foipaquestions@fbi.gov

### DOJ FOIA Request (General)
- **URL:** https://www.justice.gov/oip/make-foia-request-doj
- **Processing time:** 20 business days (simple); months for complex

### INTERPOL Records Request
- **No standard form** — written request required
- **Mail to:** Commission for the Control of INTERPOL's Files, 200 Quai Charles de Gaulle, 69006 Lyon, France
- **US DOJ INTERPOL FOIA:** Submit via FOIA.gov, select "INTERPOL — United States National Central Bureau"
- **Note:** INTERPOL does not provide standard "background checks" — use FBI Identity History Summary

## 3. DEPARTMENT OF STATE

### Form: DS-4194 — Request for Authentication Services (CONFIRMED CURRENT)
- **Agency:** US Department of State, Office of Authentications
- **URL (PDF):** https://eforms.state.gov/forms/ds4194.pdf
- **Online Form:** https://eforms.state.gov/externalForms/ds4194.aspx
- **Current version:** DS-4194 01-2023 (OMB expiration: 3/31/2026 — still valid)
- **Fee:** $20 per document
- **Payment (mail):** Check or money order payable to "U.S. Department of State" — NO cash or credit cards
- **Payment (in-person):** Credit card, debit card, contactless (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay) — NO cash/check/money order
- **Processing time:**
  - Mail: 5+ weeks
  - Walk-in drop-off: 2-3 weeks
  - Emergency appointment: Under 2 weeks (life-or-death only)

### Office of Authentications — Locations
- **Walk-in:** 600 19th Street NW, Washington, DC 20006 (7:30-9:00 AM, Mon-Thu)
- **Mailing:** PO Box 1206, Sterling, VA 20166-1206
- **Courier/FedEx:** 44132 Mercure Circle, Sterling, VA 20166

### Apostille vs. Authentication Certificate

| Feature | Apostille | Authentication Certificate |
|---|---|---|
| For countries that are... | Hague Convention members | NOT Hague members |
| Additional step needed? | No — final step | Yes — embassy legalization required |
| Fee | $20/document | $20/document |
| Form | DS-4194 | DS-4194 |

### Form: DS-11 — US Passport Application
- **URL:** https://eforms.state.gov/Forms/ds11_pdf.PDF
- **Fee:** $130 (passport book) + $35 acceptance fee
- **Note:** Not an apostille form, but passport may be needed as identity proof in authentication chains

## 4. OTHER FEDERAL AGENCIES

### USCIS — Form N-565 (Replacement Naturalization/Citizenship Document)
- **URL:** https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/forms/n-565.pdf
- **Fee:** $505 (online) / $555 (paper/mail)
- **Processing time:** 5-8 months
- **For apostille:** Get "Certified True Copy" at local USCIS office first, then DS-4194 to State Dept

### USCIS — Certified True Copy Process
- **No separate form** — make InfoPass appointment at local USCIS office
- **URL:** https://www.uscis.gov/archive/how-do-i-obtain-an-authenticated-copy-of-a-certificate-of-naturalization
- **Fee:** Free at USCIS office
- **Requirement:** Bring original + photocopy; USCIS does NOT certify by mail

### Social Security Administration — Form SS-5
- **URL:** https://www.ssa.gov/forms/ss-5.pdf
- **Fee:** FREE
- **Client data needed:** Full legal name, DOB, place of birth, citizenship, SSN, parents' names/SSNs
- **Limits:** 3 replacement cards/year, 10 per lifetime
- **For apostille:** SS cards cannot be apostilled. Request a benefit verification letter instead, notarize, then state apostille.

### IRS — Form 4506-T (Request for Transcript of Tax Return)
- **URL:** https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f4506t.pdf
- **Fee:** FREE
- **Online:** https://www.irs.gov/individuals/get-transcript
- **Processing time:** 10 business days (mail); immediate online
- **For apostille:** Transcripts need notarization first, then state SOS apostille

### IRS — Form 4506 (Request for Copy of Tax Return)
- **URL:** https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f4506.pdf
- **Fee:** $30 per tax period
- **Processing time:** Up to 75 days
- **For apostille:** IRS certified copies can go directly to US Dept of State with DS-4194

### Department of Education — Academic Credential Authentication
- **URL:** https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/replace-certify-docs/authenticate-your-document/academic-credentials.html
- **No federal form** — Dept of Ed does NOT authenticate individual diplomas
- **Process:** Diploma → notarize → state SOS apostille → embassy (if non-Hague)

### Department of Commerce — Commercial Document Authentication
- **No specific federal form**
- **Process:** Chamber of Commerce certification → state SOS apostille → DS-4194 (if needed) → embassy (if non-Hague)
- **Chamber fees:** $25-$75 per document
- **URL:** https://chamberexport.com/services/

### USDA — Phytosanitary Export Certificates
- **Forms:** PPQ Form 577 (domestic) / PPQ Form 579 (re-exported)
- **Agency:** USDA APHIS
- **URL:** https://www.aphis.usda.gov/plant-exports/certification
- **Fee:** $6 federal fee + state/county inspection ($30-$180)
- **For apostille:** Federal USDA certificates → DS-4194 to State Dept directly
