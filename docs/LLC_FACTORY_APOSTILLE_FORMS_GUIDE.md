# 1. APOSTILLE FORMS (STATE-LEVEL)

## 1A. DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

- **Issuing Agency:** Office of Notary Commissions and Authentications (ONCA), Office of the Secretary
- **Official Form:** "Request for Authentication of Documents" (Mail-In Request Form)
- **Form URL:** https://os.dc.gov/sites/default/files/dc/sites/os/publication/attachments/Mail-In%20Request%20for%20Authentications_.pdf
- **Agency Page:** https://os.dc.gov/service/authentications
- **Fee:** $15 per document
- **Processing Time:** 2-3 business days
- **Payment:** Check or money order payable to "D.C. Treasurer"
- **Office Address:** 1350 Pennsylvania Avenue NW, Suite 419, Washington, DC 20004
- **Phone:** (202) 727-6306
- **Requirements:**
  - Document must be notarized by a DC notary OR be a certified copy issued by a DC government agency
  - Completed request form (printed after online fill)
  - Pre-paid, self-addressed return envelope
- **Client Info Needed for Form:**
  - Requester full name
  - Contact information (phone, email)
  - Mailing address for return
  - Document type(s) being submitted
  - Destination country
  - Number of documents

## 1B. MARYLAND

- **Issuing Agency:** Office of the Secretary of State, Certification Desk
- **Official Form:** Certification/Apostille Request Form + Checklist
- **Checklist URL:** https://sos.maryland.gov/documents/certchecklist.pdf
- **Agency Page:** https://sos.maryland.gov/Certifications/Pages/default.aspx
- **Fee:** $5 per document (apostille or standard certification)
- **Processing Time:** Up to 1 week by mail; same-day in person
- **Payment:** U.S. check or money order payable to "Secretary of State" (mail); credit/debit card (in person)
- **Mailing Address:** Certification Desk, Office of the Secretary of State, 16 Francis Street, Annapolis, MD 21401
- **Phone:** (410) 974-5521
- **CRITICAL EXTRA STEP FOR NOTARIZED DOCUMENTS:**
  - Before submitting to SOS, the notary's signature must be authenticated by the Clerk of the Circuit Court in the county where the notary was commissioned
  - Circuit Court authentication fee: ~$1 per document (varies by county)
- **Client Info Needed for Form:**
  - Requester name and contact info
  - Document type
  - Destination country
  - Number of copies requested
  - Whether Hague (apostille) or non-Hague (standard certification)

## 1C. VIRGINIA

- **Issuing Agency:** Secretary of the Commonwealth, Authentication Division
- **Official Form:** Authentication Request Cover Letter (generated online after login/registration)
- **Cover Letter Portal:** https://www.commonwealth.virginia.gov/official-documents/authentications/how-to-submit/
- **Agency Page:** https://www.commonwealth.virginia.gov/official-documents/authentications/
- **Fee:** $10 per document (not per page); $5 each additional document if same official, same date, same country
- **Processing Time:** 5-7 business days by mail
- **In-Person:** By appointment only (Mon-Thu); appointments open each Friday at 10 AM for following week; max 10 documents per visit
- **Payment:** Check or money order payable to "The Secretary of the Commonwealth"
- **Mailing Addresses:**
  - USPS: Secretary of the Commonwealth, Authentication Division, 1111 East Broad Street, Richmond, VA 23219
  - FedEx/UPS/DHL: Secretary of the Commonwealth, Authentication Division, 400 East Cary Street, Richmond, VA 23219
- **IMPORTANT NOTES:**
  - Documents issued by VA Circuit Court, VA Dept of Health Vital Records, or State Corporation Commission go DIRECTLY to SOS -- they do NOT need notarization
  - All other documents must be notarized by a VA notary first
  - Marriage records from VA Circuit Court require a "triple seal" attached
- **Client Info Needed for Cover Letter:**
  - Requester name and contact info
  - Return mailing address
  - Document type(s)
  - Destination country
  - Number of documents
  - Payment method

# 2. FEDERAL APOSTILLE & AUTHENTICATION

## US Department of State -- Office of Authentications

- **Official Form:** DS-4194 "Request for Authentications Service"
- **Form URL (PDF):** https://eforms.state.gov/forms/ds4194.pdf
- **Online Form:** https://eforms.state.gov/externalForms/ds4194.aspx
- **Fee:** $20 per document (not per page)
- **Processing Time:** 10-11 weeks by mail; walk-in available but limited
- **Payment:** Check or money order payable to "U.S. Department of State"
- **Office Address:** 44132 Mercure Circle, PO Box 1206, Sterling, VA 20166-1206
- **Walk-in Hours:** 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM, Monday-Thursday only
- **When Required:**
  - Federal documents (FBI background checks, federal court docs, immigration docs)
  - Documents that have already been authenticated at state level and need federal-level authentication for embassy legalization (non-Hague countries)

### DS-4194 Form Fields (for auto-fill mapping):

**Section 1 -- Customer Contact Information:**
- Full name (individual) or company name
- Email address
- Phone numbers (home, work, cell)
- Mailing address (street, city, state, zip)

**Section 2 -- Courier/Representative Information:**
- Representative name (REQUIRED if someone other than customer picks up)
- Government-issued ID required for pickup

**Section 3 -- Shipping Details:**
- Delivery method
- Mailing address for return

**Section 4 -- Document Information:**
- Country (or countries) of intended use
- Number of documents
- Document type description

**Section 5 -- Projected Cost:**
- Number of documents x $20 = total

# 3. EMBASSY LEGALIZATION (NON-HAGUE COUNTRIES)

## Process Flow (Non-Hague):
1. **Notarize** document (state notary)
2. **County authentication** (MD only: Clerk of Circuit Court)
3. **State authentication** (Secretary of State / Commonwealth)
4. **Federal authentication** (US Dept of State, Form DS-4194) -- if required by embassy
5. **Embassy/Consulate legalization** (destination country's embassy)

## Embassy Legalization Notes:
- Each embassy has its own forms, fees, and requirements
- There is NO universal "embassy cover sheet" -- each embassy provides its own
- Common requirements across most embassies:
  - Original document with state apostille/authentication
  - US Dept of State authentication certificate (if required)
  - Passport copy of document owner
  - Embassy-specific application form
  - Embassy legalization fee (varies: typically $20-$100+ per document)
  - Pre-paid return envelope (if mailing)
- **LLC Factory should maintain a separate embassy requirements database** with forms/fees for the most commonly requested embassies (Saudi Arabia, UAE, China, Qatar, Egypt, etc.)

**For Apostille:** Certificate from agency -> State apostille

## 4C. Corporate Resolution

- No government form -- this is an internal company document
- Must be drafted, signed by authorized officers, and notarized
- **For Apostille:** Notarize -> (MD: Circuit Court auth) -> State apostille
- Client needs: Company name, EIN, resolution text, officer names/titles, date

## 4D. Power of Attorney

- No single government form -- drafted per client needs (General or Special POA)
- Must be notarized in the state where apostille will be obtained
- **For Apostille:** Draft POA -> Notarize -> (MD: Circuit Court auth) -> State apostille
- Client needs: Principal name/DOB/address, Agent name/DOB/address, powers granted, effective dates, destination country

**Client Info Needed:**
- Full name on certificate
- Date of birth/death/marriage
- Place of birth/death/marriage
- Mother's maiden name (birth)
- Father's name (birth)
- Relationship to registrant
- Requester's valid government ID (copy)

## 4F. Court Documents

- Certified copies obtained from the issuing court clerk
- **DC:** DC Superior Court Clerk's Office
- **MD:** Circuit Court Clerk in the relevant county
- **VA:** Circuit Court Clerk -- documents go DIRECTLY to Secretary of Commonwealth (no notarization)
- **For Apostille:** Certified court copy -> State apostille (no notarization needed for court-issued docs)

## 4G. Notarized Documents (General)

- Any private document (affidavits, contracts, agreements, etc.)
- Must be notarized by a commissioned notary in the target state
- **DC:** Notarize -> ONCA apostille
- **MD:** Notarize -> Circuit Court Clerk authentication -> SOS apostille
- **VA:** Notarize -> Secretary of Commonwealth apostille

## 4H. Academic Transcripts / Diplomas

- Request certified copies from the university Registrar's Office
- University registrar typically notarizes the document
- **The state that issues the apostille is the state where the university is located** (not the client's home state)
- Processing: 4-6 weeks typical (includes transcript request + apostille)
- Client needs: Full name on diploma, university name, graduation date, student ID (if known)

## 4I. FBI Background Checks (Identity History Summary)

- **Form:** FD-258 Fingerprint Card (standard FBI fingerprint form)
- **FBI Fee:** $18 for standard processing
- **Channeler Fee:** $30-$60 (much faster: 24-72 hours vs 2-4 weeks)
- **FBI Channelers:** Approved list at https://www.fbi.gov/how-we-can-help-you/more-fbi-services-and-information/identity-history-summary-checks
- **For Apostille:** FBI background check is a FEDERAL document -> requires US Dept of State apostille (Form DS-4194, $20)
- **Processing Note:** State Dept apostille processing is 10-11 weeks
- **Client Info Needed:**
  - Full legal name (and any prior names)
  - Date of birth
  - Social Security Number
  - Completed FD-258 fingerprint card
  - Destination country

# 6. REMOTE ONLINE NOTARIZATION (RON)

## 6A. District of Columbia

- **Status:** Law passed (DC Code 1-1231.13a) but NOT YET FULLY IMPLEMENTED as of early 2026
- **E-Notary Application:** Available online through ONCA
- **RON Interest Form:** "Remote Notary Interest Form 2026" -- submit to be notified when RON launches
- **Requirements (when active):**
  - Active DC traditional notary commission
  - Complete training via approved vendor
  - Submit e-notary application online
  - Oath by phone within 10 business days
  - Government-issued ID + KBA (knowledge-based authentication) for signers
- **Current Alternative:** Use Virginia RON (VA notary can notarize remotely for anyone anywhere)

## 6B. Maryland

- **Status:** ACTIVE -- RON fully authorized
- **Registration Portal:** https://mdsos2.my.site.com (online only; no paper form)
- **Agency Page:** https://sos.maryland.gov/Notary/Pages/RemoteNotaryInfo.aspx
- **Authorized Vendor List:** https://sos.maryland.gov/Documents/ListAuthorizedRONVendors.pdf
- **Requirements:**
  - Active MD notary commission
  - Complete approved RON training program
  - Complete remote notary notification form online
  - Select one or more authorized RON vendors
  - Confirmation email sent upon approval
- **Signer Requirements:** Government-issued ID + KBA
- **Fee:** Standard notary fee applies

## 6C. Virginia

- **Status:** ACTIVE -- Virginia was the FIRST state to authorize RON (2012)
- **Agency Page:** https://www.commonwealth.virginia.gov/official-documents/notary-commissions/
- **Guidance Memo:** https://www.governor.virginia.gov/media/governorvirginiagov/secretary-of-the-commonwealth/pdf/notary/Notarization-Guidance-Memo.pdf
- **Requirements:**
  - Active VA traditional notary commission
  - Apply for electronic notary (e-notary) endorsement
  - Use secure audio-video platform meeting VA standards
  - Notary must be physically in Virginia during session
  - Signer can be ANYWHERE in the world
- **ID Verification:** Personal knowledge, KBA, digital ID/biometrics, or credential analysis
- **Recording:** Audio-video recording of entire session mandatory (retained 5+ years)
- **Certificate Must State:** "Remote online notarization" and notary's VA location
- **Fee:** Up to $25 per electronic notarial act
- **2025 Update:** Effective July 1, 2025 -- Clerk can record any document notarized by an e-notary

# 8. CLIENT INTAKE FORMS (LLC FACTORY INTERNAL)

These are forms LLC Factory should create for internal use. Below are the recommended fields for each.

## 8A. Client Information Form

**Required Fields:**
- Client full legal name
- Business name (if applicable)
- EIN / Tax ID (if applicable)
- Mailing address
- Phone number (primary + secondary)
- Email address
- Government-issued ID type and number
- Preferred contact method
- Destination country for documents
- Is destination country a Hague Convention member? (Y/N)
- Purpose of apostille/authentication (immigration, business, education, personal)
- Urgency level (standard / expedited / rush)

## 8B. Document Submission Checklist

**Per Document Fields:**
- Document type (dropdown: birth cert, marriage cert, death cert, articles of org, good standing, POA, corporate resolution, diploma, transcript, FBI check, court doc, other)
- Document origin state (DC / MD / VA / Federal / Other)
- Is document already notarized? (Y/N)
- If notarized, in which state?
- Is document a certified copy from government agency? (Y/N)
- If MD, has Circuit Court Clerk authenticated? (Y/N)
- Number of apostille copies needed
- Hague apostille or embassy legalization?
- If embassy legalization, which embassy?

## 8C. Authorization / Release Form (Agent Authorization)

**Required Fields:**
- Client full legal name
- Client signature and date
- LLC Factory company name and address
- Scope of authorization:
  - [ ] Obtain certified copies on my behalf
  - [ ] Submit documents for notarization
  - [ ] Submit documents for apostille/authentication
  - [ ] Submit documents to US Dept of State
  - [ ] Submit documents to embassy/consulate
  - [ ] Pick up completed documents
- Duration of authorization (start date / end date)
- Notarization block (should be notarized for government agencies to accept)
- Copy of client's government-issued ID attached

## 8D. Payment Authorization Form

**Required Fields:**
- Client name
- Payment method (credit card / check / wire / cash)
- Card number / check number / wire reference
- Billing address
- Itemized service breakdown:
  - Government filing fees (itemized per document)
  - LLC Factory service fee
  - Shipping/courier fees
  - Expedited processing surcharge (if applicable)
- Total amount authorized
- Client signature and date
- Refund policy acknowledgment

# PROCESS FLOWCHARTS

## Hague Country Apostille Flow:

```
PRIVATE DOCUMENT (POA, affidavit, corporate resolution, etc.):
  Draft Document
    -> Notarize (in target state)
      -> [MD ONLY: Circuit Court Clerk authentication ~$1]
        -> Secretary of State Apostille ($5-$15)
          -> DONE -- Send to destination country

GOVERNMENT-ISSUED DOCUMENT (birth cert, court doc, business filing):
  Obtain Certified Copy from issuing agency
    -> Secretary of State Apostille ($5-$15)
      -> DONE -- Send to destination country

FEDERAL DOCUMENT (FBI check, federal court doc):
  Obtain Document from federal agency
    -> US Dept of State Apostille (DS-4194, $20)
      -> DONE -- Send to destination country
```

## Non-Hague Country (Embassy Legalization) Flow:

```
PRIVATE DOCUMENT:
  Draft Document
    -> Notarize (in target state)
```
