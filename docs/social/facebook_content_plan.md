## General Facebook Strategy Notes

- **Posting cadence:** 3-4 posts per week per page to start
- **Best posting times for DC market:** Tuesday-Thursday, 11am-1pm and 7pm-9pm
- **Engagement:** Respond to every comment within 12 hours. Like and reply to shares.
- **Facebook Groups:** Join local DC/MD/VA home improvement, interior design, and neighborhood groups. Contribute value (answer questions) before promoting.
- **Boost strategy:** Boost top-performing posts ($10-25/day) targeting DC/MD/VA homeowners, ages 30-65, interests in home improvement, interior design, and custom furniture.
- **Albums:** Use photo albums for project documentation — Facebook's algorithm favors multi-image posts.
- **Video:** Upload native video (not YouTube links) for process content. Facebook prioritizes native video in the feed.
