## Content Pillars

### 1. CNC Process
Showing the CNC router in action. Toolpath design on screen, the machine cutting, close-ups of precision edges. This content performs extremely well as short-form video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts).

**Example topics:**
- Timelapse of a full CNC cut
- Explaining what CNC means and why it matters for precision
- Side-by-side: hand cut vs. CNC cut quality
- Toolpath design walkthrough on screen
- Different bit types and what they do

### 2. Custom Builds
Documenting full projects from start to finish. Lumber selection, milling, joinery, assembly, finishing, delivery.

**Example topics:**
- Full build series (3-5 posts per project)
- Design sketches to finished piece
- Problem-solving: how we handled an unusual client request
- Scale and dimension — showing how big a piece actually is
- Hardware and joinery details

### 3. Materials & Wood Species
Educational content about the wood itself. Species characteristics, grain patterns, finishing options, sustainability.

**Example topics:**
- Wood species spotlight (walnut, oak, maple, ash, cherry)
- How humidity affects wood and why acclimation matters
- Stain vs. oil vs. clear coat — visual comparison
- Live edge vs. dimensional lumber — when to use each
- Reclaimed wood: pros, cons, and sourcing

### 4. Client Reveals
The moment a finished piece arrives in the client's space. Delivery, installation, first reactions.

**Example topics:**
- Delivery day photo/video
- Client's space before and after the piece is installed
- "Why they chose custom" — brief client story
- Designer collaboration reveals
- Repeat client projects

### 5. Workshop Life
Day-to-day content from the shop. Authentic, unpolished, human.

**Example topics:**
- Morning shop routine
- Tool maintenance and organization
- Mistakes and how we fix them
- What's currently on the bench
- Seasonal shop updates (new equipment, layout changes)

- **Instagram Feed:** 3-4 posts per week

- **Instagram Reels:** 1-2 per week (CNC and process content)
- **Facebook:** 3-4 posts per week (adapted from IG content)

### Scaling Plan
- **Month 1-2:** Establish posting rhythm, build initial content library, engage with local accounts
- **Month 3-4:** Introduce Reels consistently, start collaborating with local designers and builders
- **Month 5-6:** Add TikTok if Reels are performing well. Consider Pinterest for portfolio-style content.

## Content Production Notes

- **Photography:** Natural light preferred. Clean backgrounds. Show scale (include a hand, a coffee mug, or a person for reference).
- **Video:** Vertical (9:16) for Reels/Stories. Keep process videos under 30 seconds for Reels. Longer builds can be multi-part.
- **Editing:** Minimal. Adjust brightness and contrast. Avoid heavy filters — the wood grain and craftsmanship should be the star.
- **Audio:** For Reels, use trending audio when appropriate. Workshop ambient sound (saw, router, sanding) also performs well.
- **Batch shooting:** Dedicate 1-2 hours per week to photographing/filming current projects. Capture multiple angles and stages to create a content backlog.
