## 2. USE THESE 3 PLATFORMS TO SELL (Ranked)

| Rank | Platform | Why | Fees | Monthly Volume | Best For |
|------|----------|-----|------|---------------|----------|
| **1** | **eBay** | Largest buyer pool, best search traffic, established trust | 13.25% final value fee | Highest | Electronics, phone accessories, chargers |
| **2** | **TikTok Shop** | Fastest growing, algorithm-driven discovery, younger buyers | 5-8% commission | Growing fast | Viral products, LED items, beauty, trendy accessories |
| **3** | **Mercari** | Simple listing, good for home/lifestyle, lower competition | 10% selling fee | Moderate | Home decor, fashion accessories, watches |

### Platform Launch Order

- **Week 1**: eBay only (establish feedback, learn the flow)
- **Week 2**: Add TikTok Shop (viral potential, lower fees)
- **Week 3**: Add Mercari (diversify, catch different buyers)

### Do NOT Start With

- Poshmark (20% fee is too high for arbitrage margins)
- Amazon (requires brand approval, FBA complexity, not for beginners)
- Facebook Marketplace (local pickup friction, no shipping infrastructure)

## 3. SOURCE FROM THESE 2 PLATFORMS (Ranked)

| Rank | Platform | Why | Shipping Time | Min Order | Reliability |
|------|----------|-----|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **1** | **AliExpress** | Widest selection, established seller ratings, buyer protection | 8-20 days (standard), 5-10 days (AliExpress Choice) | 1 unit | High (use sellers with 95%+ rating, 1000+ orders) |
| **2** | **Temu** | Lower prices on trending items, faster US shipping on some items | 7-15 days | 1 unit | Medium-High (newer, but improving rapidly) |

### Sourcing Rules

- ALWAYS order a sample first before listing (verify quality)
- Use sellers with 4.7+ stars and 1,000+ orders on AliExpress
- Compare the same product across both platforms -- price difference can be 20-40%
- Factor in shipping cost to your total source cost
- Preferred shipping: AliExpress Standard Shipping or ePacket (tracking included)

### Backup Sources (When You Scale)

- **Amazon Warehouse Deals**: Open-box items at 30-50% off, domestic 2-day shipping
- **Walmart Clearance**: In-store clearance + online rollbacks, immediate availability
- **Wholesale lots (Bulq, Liquidation.com)**: Higher volume, lower per-unit cost, requires capital

## 5. FINANCIAL RULES

| Rule | Value | Rationale |
|------|-------|-----------|
| **Starting capital** | $200 | Enough for 25-40 products at $5-8 avg source cost |
| **Daily spending limit** | $30 | Prevents overbuying before proving sales velocity |
| **Minimum margin** | 35% after all fees | Below 35%, not worth the time and risk |
| **Kill threshold** | < 25% realized margin | Delist product if actual margin drops below 25% |
| **Reinvestment rate** | 70% of profit reinvested | 30% taken as income, 70% grows inventory |
| **Emergency reserve** | $50 untouchable | Covers refunds and returns without going negative |
| **Max single product risk** | $25 | Never put more than $25 into one untested product |
| **Returns budget** | 5% of revenue | Set aside for buyer returns and refund disputes |
| **Shipping supplies** | $30 initial | Poly mailers, tape, labels -- enough for 50 shipments |
| **AI/tool costs** | $5/month cap | RelistApp AI features within Empire ecosystem |

### Margin Calculator

```
Sell Price:                 $16.99
```

## 6. AUTOMATION PRIORITY

**Build first**: Automated Listing Creator

### What It Does

Takes a source product URL (AliExpress or Temu) and generates a complete, optimized listing for your selling platform in one click.

### Input
- Source product URL
- Target platform (eBay / Mercari / TikTok Shop)
- Target sell price (or "auto" for AI-recommended price)

### Output
- Optimized title (keyword-rich, platform-specific character limit)
- Product description (features, specs, shipping info)
- Category selection
- Pricing with margin calculation
- Images downloaded and background-cleaned (Stability AI)
- Listing draft ready for one-click publish

### Why This First

- Listing creation is the #1 time bottleneck (15-20 min manual vs 30 sec automated)
- Directly increases daily listing capacity from 5-10 to 50-100
- Every other feature depends on having listings live
- ROI is immediate: more listings = more sales

### Technical Scope

```
POST /api/v1/relist/listings/generate
Body: {
  "source_url": "https://aliexpress.com/item/...",
  "platform": "ebay",
  "sell_price": "auto",
  "category_hint": "phone_accessories"
}
Response: {
  "title": "iPhone 16 Pro MagSafe Clear Case...",
  "description": "...",
  "price": 12.99,
  "margin": 58,
  "images": ["url1", "url2", "url3"],
  "status": "draft"
}
```

## 9. ABORT CRITERIA

### If Day 14 Targets Are Missed

Evaluate which metrics were missed and take the corresponding action.

| Missed Metric | Diagnosis | Action |
|--------------|-----------|--------|
| Zero sales | Listings not visible or priced too high | Drop all prices 20%, improve titles with high-search keywords, add free shipping |
| < 3 sales | Products are wrong or platform algorithm not picking up listings | Switch 50% of products to different categories, relist (don't edit) to refresh algorithm |
| Sales but < 25% margin | Fees and shipping eating profit | Switch to lighter products (< 4oz), use eBay calculated shipping, source cheaper suppliers |
| High views but no sales | Price or trust issue | Lower price to lowest competitor - $0.01, add more photos, improve description |
| Low views (< 200 total) | SEO/title problem | Rewrite all titles using eBay Terapeak for keyword research, add item specifics |
| High returns (> 15%) | Product quality issue | Switch to different supplier, order samples before listing, read source reviews carefully |

### Hard Abort Triggers

Stop and reassess the entire approach if:

- **Day 30 net profit < $0** (losing money after accounting for all costs)
- **Return rate > 25%** (product quality or listing accuracy problem)
- **Platform suspension or restriction** (compliance issue, pause and fix)
- **Daily time > 4 hours with < $10 profit** (ROI on time is too low)

### Pivot Options If Aborting

1. **Switch categories entirely** (move from phone accessories to home/kitchen)
2. **Switch platforms** (if eBay not working, go TikTok Shop first)
3. **Switch source** (if AliExpress quality too low, try Amazon Warehouse)
4. **Switch model** (from drop-ship to buy-and-hold with domestic sourcing)

## 10. NEXT LEVEL

### Transition Triggers

Move from arbitrage to the next level when ALL of these are true:

| Trigger | Threshold | Why It Matters |
|---------|-----------|---------------|
| Monthly profit | > $500 consistently for 3 months | Proves the market and your execution |
| Winning products | 5+ products with 40%+ margin and 10+ monthly sales each | You have proven demand data |
| Capital available | $2,000+ reinvestment ready | Wholesale minimums require upfront capital |
| Time capacity | Current operations < 2 hrs/day (automated) | Need bandwidth for next level complexity |
| Supplier relationships | 3+ reliable AliExpress suppliers | Foundation for direct wholesale negotiation |

### Level 2: Wholesale Direct

**When**: Monthly profit > $500, 5+ proven winners

- Contact AliExpress sellers directly for wholesale pricing (50-100 unit MOQ)
- Expected cost reduction: 30-50% below single-unit price
- Buy in bulk, ship to US warehouse (your home initially)
- Ship from domestic stock = 2-3 day delivery = higher conversion
- Margin increase from 40% to 55-65%

### Level 3: Private Label

**When**: Monthly profit > $2,000, clear winning product categories

- Take your best-selling generic product
- Order with your own branding/logo (custom packaging from supplier)
- MOQ typically 200-500 units ($500-2000 initial order)
- Create brand presence on Amazon (Brand Registry)
- Margin increase to 60-75%
- Defensible business -- competitors can't copy your brand

### Level 4: SaaS (RelistApp as a Product)

**When**: RelistApp tool is mature and you're profitable

- Package RelistApp as SaaS for other resellers
- Tier pricing: Free (10/day), Pro $29/mo (100/day), Empire $79/mo (unlimited)
- Your arbitrage experience = authentic marketing and product insight
- Revenue model: recurring SaaS + your own arbitrage profits
- See `/home/rg/empire-repo/docs/relist/empire_integration.md` for full SaaS tier breakdown

### Growth Timeline

```
Month 1-3:   Arbitrage only, prove model, build data ($200-500/mo profit)
Month 4-6:   Wholesale on top 5 products, scale volume ($500-1500/mo profit)
Month 7-9:   Private label 1-2 products, brand building ($1500-3000/mo profit)
Month 10-12: SaaS launch (RelistApp), multiple revenue streams ($3000-5000/mo combined)
Year 2:      Scale all channels, hire VA for operations ($5000-10000/mo)
```
