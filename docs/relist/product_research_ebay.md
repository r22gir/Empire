## Key eBay Selling Insights for 2026

### What Drives High Sell-Through on eBay

1. **Free shipping listings** get 11% more visibility in search results
2. **30-day returns** boost search ranking significantly
3. **Multiple photos (8+)** with white background increase conversion by 25%
4. **Promoted listings** at 2-5% ad rate provides strong ROI on high-velocity items
5. **Best Offer enabled** captures price-sensitive buyers without lowering listing price

### Optimal Price Points by Category

- Electronics accessories: $8-$20 (sweet spot for impulse buys)
- Fashion: $15-$35 (below retail but above wholesale perception)
- Home & Garden: $15-$30 (functional items at "deal" pricing)
- Health & Beauty: $10-$25 (brand trust matters more than price)
- Pet Supplies: $10-$20 (pet owners spend freely)

### Timing Matters

- **January:** Organization/storage, fitness equipment, healthy eating
- **February-March:** Spring cleaning, outdoor prep, gardening
- **April-June:** Outdoor living, pool/beach, travel accessories
- **July-August:** Back-to-school, dorm room essentials
- **September-November:** Holiday gift prep, home decor
- **December:** Gift cards, stocking stuffers, last-minute gifts
