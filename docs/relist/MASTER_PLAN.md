# RelistApp Arbitrage Master Plan
## 1000 Sales/Day Drop-Ship Strategy

---

## Executive Summary

**Opportunity:** Drop-ship arbitrage — buy products on cheaper platforms (Amazon, Walmart, AliExpress), relist on higher-traffic platforms (eBay, Mercari, TikTok Shop) at markup. Source seller ships directly to buyer. You never touch inventory.

**Revenue projections:**
- Conservative: 200 sales/day × $1.50 avg profit = $300/day = $9,000/month
- Moderate: 500 sales/day × $1.00 avg profit = $500/day = $15,000/month  
- Aggressive: 1000 sales/day × $0.75 avg profit = $750/day = $22,500/month

**Capital required:** $500 minimum, $2,000 comfortable, $5,000 for fast scaling
**Time to profitability:** 14-21 days
**Key risk:** Account suspension — mitigated by multi-platform diversification and compliance

---

## The Model

### How It Works
1. **SCOUT** — Find product on Amazon at $8.99
2. **CALCULATE** — Same product sells on eBay for $14.99. After eBay fees (13.6%) = $12.95 net. Profit = $3.96
3. **LIST** — Create eBay listing with Amazon's photos/description (optimized for eBay SEO)
4. **SELL** — Customer buys on eBay for $14.99
5. **ORDER** — Buy on Amazon for $8.99, ship to eBay customer's address
6. **PROFIT** — Keep $3.96 after all fees

### Why It Works at Scale
- Thousands of products have $1-5 price spreads between platforms
- At 1000 items, even $0.50 average profit = $500/day
- No inventory risk — you only buy after you sell
- Infinite "warehouse" — you list items you don't own
- RelistApp automates 90% of the manual work

### The Math
```
1000 listings active
× 3% daily sell-through rate = 30 sales/day (starting)
Ramp to 5000 listings = 150 sales/day
Ramp to 20,000 listings = 600+ sales/day
Target: 50,000+ active listings = 1000+ sales/day
```

## Risk Management (Top 5)

1. **Account suspension** — Diversify across 5+ platforms. Never >40% revenue on one platform. Maintain >98% positive feedback. See `account_health_playbook.md`.
2. **Price changes** — Real-time price monitoring. Auto-pause listings if margin drops below $0.25. Daily repricing.
3. **Returns eating profit** — Avoid categories with >10% return rate. Auto-delist products with >8% returns. Refund <$10 items without return.
4. **Cash flow crunch** — $2K reserve. Never invest >30% of capital in one day. Track float daily.
5. **Legal/tax** — Form LLC before $10K/month. Use TaxJar for sales tax. Quarterly estimated payments. See `tax_legal_compliance.md`.
