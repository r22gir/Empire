## BSR (Best Sellers Rank) to Sales Volume Guide

Understanding what BSR numbers mean in terms of daily sales:

| BSR Range | Estimated Daily Sales | Monthly Volume |
|-----------|----------------------|----------------|
| 1-100 | 50-300+ units/day | 1,500-9,000+ |
| 100-500 | 10-50 units/day | 300-1,500 |
| 500-2,000 | 5-15 units/day | 150-450 |
| 2,000-5,000 | 2-8 units/day | 60-240 |
| 5,000-10,000 | 1-3 units/day | 30-90 |
| 10,000-50,000 | 0.5-1 unit/day | 15-30 |

**Note:** BSR is relative per category and updates hourly. Categories with more listings (e.g., Clothing) require lower BSR for same volume vs smaller categories (e.g., Office Products).

## Key Takeaways for RelistApp Sourcing

1. **Consumables win**: Products people use up and reorder (poop bags, wipes, ink, supplements) provide the most reliable arbitrage volume
2. **New phone cycle = gold**: Every new iPhone/Samsung launch creates 3-6 months of peak accessory demand
3. **TikTok drives discovery**: Products that go viral on TikTok (Pink Stuff, Fullstar Chopper, INABA cat treats) maintain elevated sales for 6-12 months
4. **Price varies by channel**: The same product often has $2-8 price differences between Amazon, Walmart, Costco, and eBay
5. **Under $25 sweet spot**: Products in the $8-25 range have the highest velocity and lowest buyer resistance
