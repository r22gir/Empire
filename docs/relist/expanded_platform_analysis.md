## Tier 1: High Volume Platforms (Launch Day)

### 1. eBay
**Priority: P0 -- Launch Day**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Final Value Fee**: 13.6% without store / 12.7% with Basic Store + $0.30 (under $10) or $0.40 (over $10) per order
- **Insertion fee**: First 250-300 free/month (depends on store tier), then $0.35 each
- **Promoted Listings**: Optional 2-8% ad rate (pay only on sale)
- **International fee**: +1.65% on international sales
- **Store subscription**: $0 (no store), $7.95/mo (Starter), $21.95/mo (Basic), $59.95/mo (Premium)
- **No separate payment processing fee** (included in FVF since Managed Payments)

#### Best Categories
Electronics accessories, Home & Garden, Auto Parts, Collectibles, Clothing, Sporting Goods

#### Daily Volume Potential
High. eBay has 132M+ active buyers globally. A store with 1,000 listings can realistically generate 50-200 sales/day depending on category and pricing.

#### Difficulty
Medium. New seller limits (10 items/$500 initially) require calling eBay to increase. Algorithm rewards sales velocity, so start pricing aggressively. Returns management is critical -- keep defect rate below 2%.

#### Key Strategy
- Use Terapeak for sold-item research before listing
- Fill ALL item specifics (eBay rewards complete listings with search visibility)
- Start Promoted Listings at 2%, increase on winners
- Build seller reputation fast -- first 100 feedback is critical
- Price 5-10% below competition initially to build velocity

### 2. Mercari
**Priority: P0 -- Launch Day**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Selling fee**: 10% flat on item price + buyer-paid shipping
- **Payment processing**: None for sellers (removed January 2026)
- **Withdrawal**: Free via direct deposit; $3 for Instant Pay
- **Authentication**: Optional $5 per item ($10 for digital certificate)
- **Effective total**: ~10%

#### Best Categories
Fashion, Electronics, Home goods, Toys, Beauty, Video Games

#### Daily Volume Potential
Medium. Mercari has a strong mobile-first user base. With 500+ listings, expect 20-80 sales/day. Platform favors fresh listings and active sellers.

#### Difficulty
Easy. No selling limits for new accounts. Quick setup, simple listing process. Mobile-friendly. The 3-day auto-rating system means fast feedback accumulation.

#### Key Strategy
- List daily to stay active in the algorithm
- Use Smart Pricing to allow price drops over time
- Relist stale items every 7-14 days for fresh visibility
- Respond to offers within 24 hours
- Ship same-day or next-day (impacts seller rating)

### 3. Facebook Marketplace
**Priority: P0 -- Launch Day**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Shipped items**: 10% of total (item + shipping + tax), minimum $0.80
- **Local pickup**: 0% (no fee)
- **Shipping labels**: No longer provided by FB (as of Feb 2025) -- use PirateShip
- **Boosted listings**: $1-10/day optional advertising

#### Best Categories
Furniture, Home goods, Electronics, Vehicles, Local services, Baby items

#### Daily Volume Potential
Medium. Massive reach through Facebook's 3B+ users, but conversion is lower than dedicated marketplaces. Local pickup eliminates shipping and fees entirely. With shipping enabled, 15-60 sales/day is achievable.

#### Difficulty
Easy for local. Medium for shipped. No dedicated seller dashboard -- interface is consumer-grade. Scam messages are rampant. No formal seller protection program comparable to eBay.

#### Key Strategy
- Prioritize local pickup for zero-fee sales on bulky items
- Use shipping for lightweight, high-margin items
- Join and cross-post to local buy/sell groups
- Respond to messages fast (FB rewards responsiveness)
- Use PirateShip for cheapest shipping labels

### 4. Amazon
**Priority: P1 -- Week 2**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Individual plan**: $0.99/item sold (no monthly fee)
- **Professional plan**: $39.99/month (no per-item fee)
- **Referral fee**: 8-15% depending on category
- **FBA fees**: Pick & pack from ~$3.22/unit + storage ($0.87-2.40/cu ft/month)
- **Payment processing**: Included in referral fee

#### Best Categories
Everything -- Amazon dominates all categories. Best arbitrage opportunities in Home & Kitchen, Beauty, Toys, Office, Pet Supplies.

#### Daily Volume Potential
Very High. Amazon has 300M+ active customers. A well-optimized listing can generate 100-500+ sales/day. But competition is fierce and margins are thin.

#### Difficulty
Hard. Requires brand approval for many categories. Listing against existing ASINs means competing on price. FBA logistics add complexity. Account suspension risk is real. Retail arbitrage (buying from other retailers to sell on Amazon) is technically allowed but risky with brand gating.

#### Key Strategy
- Use FBA for Buy Box advantage
- Focus on ungated categories initially
- Monitor BSR and use Keepa/CamelCamelCamel for price history
- Avoid restricted brands
- Consider as a SOURCE rather than a destination for arbitrage

### 5. TikTok Shop
**Priority: P1 -- Week 2**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Referral fee**: 6% standard (3% promotional rate for first 30 days)
- **Payment processing**: ~2.9% + fixed fee
- **Fulfillment**: FBT (Fulfilled by TikTok) required for all US sellers as of March 31, 2026
- **Affiliate commissions**: 10-20% if using creators for promotion
- **Effective total**: ~9% without affiliates, 19-29% with affiliates

#### Best Categories
Viral gadgets, Beauty & Skincare, Fashion, Home gadgets, Wellness products, Impulse buys under $30

#### Daily Volume Potential
High potential but volatile. Single viral video can drive 10,000+ orders. Average steady-state with good content: 20-100 sales/day. Discovery-based platform means inconsistent daily volumes.

#### Difficulty
Medium-Hard. Requires video content creation or affiliate partnerships. FBT fulfillment adds logistics complexity. New policy (March 2026): independent shipping no longer allowed -- must use FBT, Upgraded TikTok Shipping, or CBT.

#### Key Strategy
- Source viral-friendly products (must be demonstrable in 15-second video)
- Partner with micro-influencers (10-20% affiliate commission)
- Use FBT for fulfillment compliance
- Target impulse-buy price points ($10-30)
- Ride trending sounds/hashtags for organic reach

## Tier 2: Niche/Growing Platforms (Week 2-4)

### 6. Etsy
**Priority: P1 -- Week 2**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Listing fee**: $0.20 per listing (renews every 4 months or on sale)
- **Transaction fee**: 6.5% on total order (including shipping)
- **Payment processing**: 3% + $0.25 (US)
- **Offsite Ads**: 12-15% (mandatory for sellers over $10K/year, opt-in under $10K)
- **Effective total**: ~12-15% for most sellers

#### Best Categories
Handmade goods, Vintage (20+ years old), Craft supplies, Digital downloads (SVG, PNG, templates, CNC files), Printables, Custom items

#### Daily Volume Potential
Medium. Etsy has 90M+ active buyers specifically looking for unique/handmade/vintage items. 10-50 sales/day with a well-stocked shop. Digital products can sell on autopilot.

#### Difficulty
Medium. Etsy requires items to be handmade, vintage, or craft supplies -- standard retail arbitrage is against ToS. However, DIGITAL PRODUCTS are a massive opportunity. Offsite Ads fee is mandatory and significant for successful sellers.

#### Key Strategy
- Focus on digital products for infinite-margin, zero-shipping revenue
- SVG/CNC files are the sweet spot for CraftForge integration
- Use Etsy SEO: long-tail keywords in titles and tags
- Free shipping threshold ($35+) boosts search ranking
- Avoid Offsite Ads by keeping under $10K/year or accept the 12% cost

### 7. Walmart Marketplace
**Priority: P2 -- Month 2**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Setup/monthly/listing fees**: None
- **Referral fee**: 6-15% depending on category
- **WFS (Walmart Fulfillment Services)**: From $3.45/unit (optional)
- **New Seller Savings**: Up to $75,000 in value for sellers going live after Feb 1, 2026
- **Effective total**: 6-15% (among the lowest for a major marketplace)

#### Best Categories
Home & Garden, Electronics, Toys, Grocery, Health & Wellness, Automotive

#### Daily Volume Potential
High. Walmart.com has 120M+ monthly visitors. Less competition than Amazon. With approval and established listings, 30-150 sales/day is achievable.

#### Difficulty
Hard (approval process). Walmart has strict seller requirements: established ecommerce track record, US business tax ID, warehouse/fulfillment capability. Approval can take 2-4 weeks. Once approved, selling is straightforward. No monthly fees make it very attractive.

#### Key Strategy
- Apply early -- approval takes time
- Highlight eBay/Amazon sales history in application
- Start with WFS for algorithm advantage (similar to FBA)
- Use the New Seller Savings ($75K value) aggressively
- Price match or beat Amazon on key items

### 8. Poshmark
**Priority: P0 -- Launch Day**

#### Fees (2026)
- **Under $15**: Flat $2.95 fee
- **$15 and above**: 20% commission
- **Shipping**: Paid by buyer via flat-rate USPS label (~$7.67 for up to 5 lbs)
- **Payment processing**: Included in commission
- **No listing fees**

#### Best Categories
Women's fashion, Men's fashion, Shoes, Handbags, Jewelry, Home decor, Beauty

#### Daily Volume Potential
Low-Medium. Poshmark's social-commerce model requires active engagement (sharing, following, Posh Parties). With consistent sharing, 10-40 sales/day. Best for fashion-focused inventory.

#### Difficulty
Medium. Requires daily closet sharing (30 min minimum). Social engagement drives visibility. 20% fee is the highest among major platforms -- price accordingly. Returns limited to 3 days.

#### Key Strategy
- Share entire closet 2-3 times daily
- Participate in Posh Parties for category-specific exposure
- Bundle items for higher AOV (5 lbs same shipping cost)
- Price 20-25% higher than other platforms to offset the 20% fee

## Digital Products Section: Zero Shipping, Infinite Margin

### The Digital Products Opportunity

Digital products represent the highest-margin opportunity in the RelistApp ecosystem. Zero inventory, zero shipping, infinite scalability. Once created, a digital product sells forever with no additional cost.

**Profit margins: 85-95%** (only platform fees reduce your margin)

### Best Platforms for Digital Products

| Platform | Fee on Digital | Best Digital Product Types | Monthly Revenue Potential |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Etsy** | ~12-15% total | SVG/CNC files, printables, templates, patterns | $500-5,000+/month |
| **Shopify** | ~3% (Shopify Payments) | Courses, templates, software, CNC files | $1,000-10,000+/month |
| **Gumroad** | 10% | eBooks, courses, software, art packs | $200-2,000/month |
| **Creative Market** | 40% (they take a lot) | Design assets, fonts, templates | $300-3,000/month |

### SVG/CNC Files -- CraftForge Integration

This is a natural fit for Empire Workroom and WoodCraft. The CNC and design expertise already exists in-house through CraftForge.

**What Sells (2026 research):**
- CNC project files (furniture plans, signs, ornaments, boxes)
- SVG cut files for Cricut/Silhouette (home decor, wedding, seasonal)
- Laser engraving files (coasters, keychains, name signs)
- 3D print STL files (functional prints, decor, organizers)
- Sublimation-ready PNG bundles (20-file packs at $5-12)

**Revenue Benchmarks (Etsy):**
- Average digital product shop: ~$2,444/month revenue
- Average yearly revenue: ~$29,324
- Profit margins: 90%+ (only Etsy fees reduce margin)
- Top sellers: $10,000+/month in digital downloads

**Critical 2026 Policy Change:**
Etsy now requires CNC/laser/Cricut items to use the seller's OWN ORIGINAL DESIGNS -- not purchased templates from other creators. This is actually an advantage: Empire Workroom already creates original designs, creating a moat against low-effort resellers.

**Estimated passive income: $2,000-5,000/month** once 100+ digital products are listed and optimized. This is achievable within 60-90 days of focused effort.

### Printables and Templates

**Top sellers in 2026:**
- Notion templates ($5-25 each) -- business, finance, project management
- Wedding planning printables ($10-40 bundles)
- Budget trackers and financial planners ($5-15)
- Social media templates (Canva-compatible, $10-30 packs)
- Resume templates ($10-20)

### AI-Powered Digital Products (Emerging 2026)

The fastest-growing digital product category:
- AI prompt packs ($5-15) -- curated prompts for ChatGPT, Midjourney, Claude
- Automation templates ($10-50) -- Zapier, Make.com, n8n workflows
- AI content resource bundles ($15-40)

### Digital Products Action Plan

**Week 1-2:**
- Audit existing Empire Workroom CNC designs for digital sale potential
- Create 20 SVG/CNC file listings on Etsy
- Set up Shopify store for digital products (or add digital section to existing)

**Week 3-4:**
- Expand to 50 digital listings
- Create 5 template/printable products (budget planners, project trackers)
- Test AI prompt packs on Etsy and Gumroad

**Month 2+:**
- Scale to 200+ digital products
- Create product bundles (10-pack CNC file collections)
- Build email list from digital product buyers
- Target: $2,000/month passive digital income by Month 3
