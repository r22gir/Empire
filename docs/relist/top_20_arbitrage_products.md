## Key Insights

- **16 of 20** top products source best from AliExpress

- Average profit per unit from Walmart: **$4.00** (only works for brand-name products)
- AliExpress downside: 15-30 day shipping requires inventory buffer or customer expectation management

### Portfolio Strategy Recommendation

**Tier 1 -- Start Here (LOW risk, $10+ profit/unit):**
- USB-C Hub, Wireless Mouse, Laptop Stand, Kitchen Utensil Set, Massage Gun

**Tier 2 -- Scale Into (LOW risk, $6-$10 profit/unit):**
- LED Strip Lights, USB-C Cables, Dog Leash, Dog Bowl, Cat Litter Mat, Screen Protectors

**Tier 3 -- Test Carefully (MEDIUM risk or lower margin):**
- Baby Bottle Warmer, Compression Leggings, Neck Fan, CeraVe bundles, Pink Stuff bundles

### Total Portfolio Potential

Running all 20 products at 10 units/day each:
- **Conservative Monthly Revenue:** $66,000-$70,000
- **Conservative Monthly Profit:** $65,000-$135,000 (gross, before overhead)
- **Realistic target (5 products, 5 units/day each):** $8,000-$15,000/month profit

### Risk Mitigation

1. **AliExpress shipping time:** Order inventory in bulk, ship to your location first, then fulfill from US. Adds $3-5 shipping but enables fast delivery.
2. **Returns:** Budget 5-8% return rate into margin calculations. Electronics and fashion have highest return rates.
3. **eBay account limits:** New accounts start with 10 items/$500/month. Scale gradually.
4. **Brand restrictions:** Some brands (Nike, Apple accessories) have VERO complaints on eBay. Stick to generic/white-label for safety.
5. **Competition:** Monitor pricing weekly. If margins drop below $5/unit, rotate to new products.
