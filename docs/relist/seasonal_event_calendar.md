# RelistApp - 2026 Seasonal & Event Calendar

> Month-by-month arbitrage strategy with top categories, volume multipliers, pricing tactics, and inventory pre-positioning.

---

## How to Read This Calendar

- **Volume Multiplier**: How much sales volume increases vs a normal week (1.0x = baseline)
- **Prep Window**: When to start sourcing and listing BEFORE the event
- **Price Strategy**: Whether to price premium (demand > supply) or competitive (high competition)
- **Pre-Position**: What to source and list before the rush hits

## July 2026 - Amazon Prime Day (BIGGEST ARBITRAGE MONTH)

**Key Events**: Amazon Prime Day (expected July 14-15), Independence Day (Jul 4), summer peak

**Volume Multiplier**: 2.5x (Prime Day week: 4.0x)

### Top 5 Categories

| # | Category | Why It Sells | Source Price | Sell Price | Platform |
|---|----------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Electronics (earbuds, chargers, smart home) | Prime Day deals → relist at profit | $8-25 | $25-50 | eBay |
| 2 | Phone accessories (cases, screen protectors) | New phone buyers from Prime deals | $1-4 | $10-18 | eBay |
| 3 | Smart home devices (plugs, bulbs, cameras) | Deep Prime discounts → arbitrage gold | $10-20 | $28-45 | Mercari |
| 4 | Kitchen gadgets | Impulse Prime purchases → resell surplus | $5-15 | $18-35 | eBay |
| 5 | Beauty tech (hair tools, skincare devices) | Prime deals → gift market | $8-20 | $25-45 | TikTok Shop |

### Prime Day Arbitrage Strategy

```
BEFORE Prime Day (July 1-13):
  - Set up price tracking on 50 target Amazon products
  - Pre-create listing templates (title, description, images ready)
  - Ensure all selling platform accounts are in good standing
  - Clear old inventory to free up capital

DURING Prime Day (July 14-15):
  - Monitor Amazon deals every 30 minutes
  - Buy products discounted 40%+ with proven resale demand
  - List within 1 hour of purchase on eBay/Mercari
  - Price at 80% of pre-Prime retail (still profit, undercuts retail)
  - Use "Lightning Deal" urgency in listing titles

AFTER Prime Day (July 16-31):
  - Continue listing purchased inventory
  - Monitor which items sell fastest -- double down
  - Handle shipping and fulfillment (ship within 24 hours)
  - Analyze margins and update playbook for October Prime Day
```

### Pricing Strategy
- Price at 70-80% of pre-sale retail price
- Buyers who missed Prime Day will pay above Prime price but below full retail
- Volume over margin -- aim for 30%+ margin, prioritize sell-through speed

### Inventory Pre-Positioning
- Allocate 50-70% of monthly capital budget to Prime Day purchasing
- Pre-position $500-2000 for rapid buying on July 14-15
- Have shipping supplies stocked (boxes, poly mailers, tape, labels)
