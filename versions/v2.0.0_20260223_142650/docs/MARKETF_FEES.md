# MarketF Fee Structure

## Overview

MarketF charges **only 8% marketplace fees** - significantly lower than eBay's 12.9%. This means more money in your pocket on every sale!

## Fee Breakdown

### Marketplace Fee: 8%
This is our platform fee for listing and selling on MarketF. It covers:
- Platform maintenance and hosting
- Customer support
- Fraud protection
- Dispute resolution
- Payment processing infrastructure

### Payment Processing Fee: 2.9% + $0.30
This is Stripe's fee for processing credit card payments. This is standard across all payment platforms.

## No Hidden Fees

Unlike other platforms, MarketF has NO:
- ❌ Listing fees
- ❌ Insertion fees
- ❌ Category fees
- ❌ Optional feature fees
- ❌ Store subscription fees (basic tier)

## Escrow & Payment Timeline

1. **Buyer Pays** - Funds held in escrow
2. **You Ship** - Mark as shipped with tracking
3. **Delivered** - 48-hour waiting period
4. **Payout** - Funds released to your account

**Typical Timeline:** Receive payment 3-5 days after delivery

## Payment Methods

We payout via:
- Direct bank transfer (ACH) - Free
- Instant transfer - 1% fee (max $10)

## Refunds & Disputes

If a buyer requests a refund:
- You can approve and issue full refund
- If disputed, our team reviews the case
- Partial refunds available for damaged items
- Sellers protected from fraudulent claims
