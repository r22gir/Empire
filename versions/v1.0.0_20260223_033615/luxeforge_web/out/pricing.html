Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Billing is prorated automatically.

Does LuxeForge work for both drapery and roman shades?

Yes. The pricing engine supports per-width and per-sqft calculations simultaneously, so you can mix product types on a single quote.

Are the Rowley and Kravet catalogs included?

The catalogs are available on Pro and Enterprise plans. Fabric and hardware markups are configurable per product or globally.

Is my client data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We are SOC 2 compliant and never sell your data.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Annual plans receive a 20% discount.
