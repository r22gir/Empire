# MarketF Seller Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Seller Account

1. Sign up at [marketf.com/signup](https://marketf.com/signup)
2. Verify your email address
3. Complete your profile
4. Connect your Stripe account for payments

### Step 2: Create Your First Listing

#### Required Information:
- **Title** - Clear, descriptive (max 255 characters)
- **Description** - Detailed condition and features
- **Category** - Select the most relevant category
- **Condition** - New, Like New, Good, Fair, or Poor
- **Price** - Your asking price
- **Photos** - At least 1, up to 8 recommended
- **Package Dimensions** - Required for shipping calculation
- **Ships From ZIP** - Your shipping location

#### Best Practices:
- ✅ Use all 8 photo slots
- ✅ Include close-ups of any flaws
- ✅ Write detailed descriptions
- ✅ Be honest about condition
- ✅ Price competitively

### Step 3: Ship Your Orders

When an item sells, you have 2 options:

#### Option A: Ship with ShipForge (Recommended)
1. Go to your seller dashboard
2. Click "Ship with ShipForge" on the order
3. Package details auto-fill from your listing
4. Choose carrier and service level
5. Purchase label
6. Tracking automatically updates the order

#### Option B: Ship on Your Own
1. Package the item
2. Print shipping label from your carrier
3. In your dashboard, click "Mark Shipped"
4. Enter tracking number and carrier
5. Ship the package

**Shipping Timeline:**
- Ship within 1-2 business days
- Use tracking for all shipments
- Package items securely

## Managing Your Store

### Dashboard Overview

Your seller dashboard shows:
- **Revenue This Month** - Total earnings
- **Active Listings** - Number of live products
- **Rating** - Your seller rating
- **Orders to Ship** - Action items
- **Recent Sales** - Sales history

### Inventory Management

**Active Listings:**
- Edit pricing anytime
- Update photos/description
- Mark as sold manually if needed

**Sold Items:**
- Automatic when order is placed
- Cannot be relisted (create new listing)

**Draft Listings:**
- Save incomplete listings
- Publish when ready

## Pricing Strategies

### Competitive Pricing
- Research similar items on MarketF and eBay
- Price 5-10% below eBay to account for lower fees
- Use "Make Offer" for negotiable items

### Pricing Calculator
```
Desired Profit: $50
+ Marketplace Fee (8%): $4.34
+ Payment Fee (2.9%): $1.58
+ Your Cost: $20
─────────────────────────
List Price: $75.92 → $76
```

### Offers Feature
Enable "Make Offer" and set a minimum:
- Buyers can submit offers
- Auto-accept, auto-decline, or review manually
- Great for moving inventory faster

## Customer Service

### Responding to Messages
- Reply within 24 hours
- Be professional and friendly
- Answer questions honestly

### Handling Returns
- Clearly state your return policy in listings
- 30-day returns recommended
- Buyer pays return shipping (unless item not as described)

### Disputes
If a buyer opens a dispute:
1. Respond within 48 hours
2. Provide evidence (photos, tracking)
3. Try to resolve directly with buyer
4. MarketF support will mediate if needed

## Building Your Reputation

### Get 5-Star Reviews
- Ship quickly (1-2 days)
- Package items securely
- Describe items accurately
- Communicate promptly
- Go above and beyond

### Seller Metrics
Your rating is based on:
- Review ratings (1-5 stars)
- Shipping speed
- Response time
- Return rate

## Cross-Posting from MarketForge

Already using MarketForge? List items on both platforms with one click:

1. Create listing in MarketForge
2. Select "MarketF" as a publishing platform
3. Listing appears on both platforms
4. Inventory syncs automatically

## Advanced Tips

### Photography Tips
- Natural lighting preferred
- Plain background
- Show all angles
- Include measurements
- Close-ups of flaws/defects

### SEO Optimization
- Use relevant keywords in title
- Include brand, model, size
- Accurate categorization
- Complete all fields

### Seasonal Strategies
- List seasonal items 2-3 months ahead
- Holiday items in October/November
- Back-to-school items in July
- Summer items in April/May

## Payment & Taxes

### Getting Paid
- Funds released 48 hours after delivery
- Paid directly to your Stripe account
- Transfer to bank account (1-2 days)
- Instant transfer available (1% fee)

### Tax Reporting
- MarketF provides annual 1099-K if you meet thresholds
- Keep records of all sales
- Consult a tax professional

## Seller Policies

### Prohibited Items
- Counterfeit goods
- Stolen items
- Illegal substances
- Weapons
- Recalled products

### Seller Protection
You're protected from:
- Item not received (if you have tracking)
- Fraudulent chargebacks
- Buyer's remorse (with no-return policy)
